TRIBES OF THE ENDLESS DUST

A post-apocalyptic, hex-based 4X. Rebuild civilization on a dying world that is actively trying to kill you — while four other warlords do the same.

EXPLORE FACTIONS SEE THE SPEC

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT

Five differentiators from the usual 4X grind.

  • The world fights back. Radiation drifts. Dust storms move. The Bloom reclaims your tiles. The map is the sixth player.
  • Asymmetric factions. Five tribes, each with unique units, tech branch, and win condition. No mirror matchups.
  • Diplomacy is real but fragile. Alliances cost something. Betrayal pays something. The game remembers.
  • Heroes persist. Your Warlord levels up across matches and carries equipment between games.
  • Mobile-first. 10–20 minute matches. One-thumb playable. No tooltips that require hover.

THE FIVE TRIBES

Each faction is a different game. Pick the one that punishes the opponent you fear most.

RECLAIMERS

The Gardeners

"We are gardeners with rifles."

Druids who weaponize the Bloom. Cover 40% of the map in fungus to win. Bloom doesn't hurt them — it feeds them.

SCRAPPERS

The Tinkerers

"Everything broken can kill again."

Junk-tech salvagers. Win at 500 Scrap and 6 cities. Every kill returns half the Scrap. Ruins give double loot.

THE CHOIR

The Cult

"The signal called. We answered."

Psionic cult around a dead satellite. Win by broadcasting from 3 Relay Towers for 5 turns. See all enemy warlords.

HOLLOW LEGION

The Order

"Discipline is the cure for hope."

Pre-collapse military remnant. Conquer every capital. Lean economy on Power, +1 def per adjacent friendly unit.

DRIFTERS

The Nomads

"Roots are for the dead."

Mobile Camps instead of cities. Win by walking your warlord across every enemy's starting quadrant. +1 move always.
